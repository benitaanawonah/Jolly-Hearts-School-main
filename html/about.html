About Us

About Jolly Hearts School

Jolly heart Montessori school is like your child’s extended family. Our nursery and primary settings have been carefully setup to provide intimacy, love and safety for our children. “The essential thing is to arouse such an interest that it engages the child’s whole personality”- Maria Montessori. Our children aged 2-6 are taught using the authentic Montessori approach by loving and experienced teachers using a full range of Montessori materials. We also offer part time specialist Montessori teacher training for interested would be Montessori teachers. At Jolly hearts, we are committed to ensuring the positive and progressive growth of our children in a loving environment.

Learn More

  • History

    Welcome to Jolly Hearts School a truly condusive and special place to learn. We opened our doors to students in August 2016 with twelve pupils in enrolment, four of whom were staff children . That years Jolly Hearts school thrilled audience to an amazing Christmas/end of year concert that showed off our student’s skills in confidence, communication and creativity. By January 2017, Jolly Hearts school had doubled her population and since then has not stopped growing by God’s grace.

  • What does Jolly Hearts offer to the pupils?

    Jolly Hearts school offers educational stability and continuity as we cater for a diverse age group( 18 months- 11 years). We ensure each has a voice and varied opportunities to excel by maintaining a very healthy teacher pupil ratio. Jolly Hearts school gives her pupils varied opportunities to learn . We organize many programs such as different themes for each term, National day, End of year concert, Valentine’s Day, Excursions, Occupational day and so on.

  • Our Admission Process

    Admission procedures begin every year in August and recommendation into classes is done strictly by age and a conducted test on the child’s academical performance. The school admits from 18 months of age to 11 years.

  • Our Health Policy

    Each pupil and member of staff are required to tender a medical report at the point of entry along side with the registration form, this is to ensure safety of every member of our school community. Parents are to co-operate with the school as regard this.
