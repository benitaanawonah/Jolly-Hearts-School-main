Our Curriculum

  • What does Jolly Heart Curriculum look like?

    We run a rich and customized curriculum, tailored after Montessori methods elements of the Nigerian Curriculum, Head Start Program (U.S.A) English National Curriculum which elevates the pride and esteem of every child. Our students are guided as they explore, discover, develop skills and form opinions about how the world works. They are able to apply developed skills for real life situations. .

  • What academic materials does my child get?

    All students are offered leading text books with content delivered by highly qualified and experienced teachers who are committed to their students. Personalized reading is also encouraged. Every class has a mini library with books that catch the children’s fantasy such as Encyclopedia, McGraw-Hill, Harcourt Florida Edition in most subjects, fiction and non fiction, Tarzan, Barbie, Ninja turtles just to mention a few

  • Does Jolly Heart school offer extra- curricular activities?

    All students at Jolly Heart School are required to offer swimming which is free; students may be exempted from from swimming class on medical performing arts and special reading class as co-curriculum every term. Asides from the co-curricular activities there are extra-curricular activities on offer. It includes Ballet dancing, Arts and Design.

  • What learning area does Jolly Hearts School focus on?

    Our standard is truly international quality. There are four important principles expectations, progression, engagement and transformation. There is also a strong sense of discipline within the school, focused on developing self-discipline in each student. Discipline is based on mutual respect.
